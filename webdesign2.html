Case Study: Dr. Gena Ross Ministries

Enhancing Usability & Spiritual Engagement Through UX Design

The Problem

Dr. Ross's website had low engagement and poor usability, despite her impactful content. Key issues:

  • Cluttered layout with weak visual hierarchy.
  • Redundant navigation confusing users.
  • No clear CTAs to guide visitors toward books, videos, or contact.
  • Broken Links: Several internal links led to 404 errors.
  • Unclear Value Proposition: Visitors couldn't quickly grasp Dr. Ross's core mission (e.g., "Is this a ministry site? A book promo site? A personal blog?").
  • Brand Consistency Gaps: Mix of low-resolution and cropped photos.
  • Analytics & Maintenance Challenges: Dr. Ross wasn't trained to update SEO metadata or alt text post-launch.
  • No easy way for followers to schedule spiritual counseling sessions.

Real-user struggle: Visitors couldn’t find CTA button to buy the books.

The Goals

Redesign the website to:

  • Fix Structural & Usability Issues
    • Problem: Cluttered layout, weak hierarchy
    • Goal: Implement grid-based design with clear visual hierarchy (Fitts' Law for CTAs)
    • Problem: Redundant navigation
    • Goal: Consolidate menu items into intuitive categories (e.g., "Media" for all videos)
    • Problem: Broken links
    • Goal: Audit and fix 100% of internal links pre-launch
  • Clarify Messaging & Purpose
    • Problem: Unclear value proposition
    • Goal: Add hero section with tagline + 3-key offerings (e.g., "Inspirator • Motivator • Evangelist")
    • Problem: Brand inconsistency
    • Goal: Standardize imagery (curate 5 high-res photos) and add minimalist logo
  • Drive Conversions
    • Problem: No clear CTAs
    • Goal: Design 3 primary buttons ("Buy Books," "Watch Sermons," "Contact") above the fold
    • Problem: No scheduling system
    • Goal: Implement Calendly integration for 30-minute spiritual sessions
  • Ensure Sustainability
    • Problem: Analytics/maintenance gaps
    • Goal: Train client on SEO/metadata updates (1-hr video tutorial)
    • Goal: Install Google Analytics event tracking for CTAs

Feature Highlights

The Calendly integration successfully automated Dr. Ross's scheduling, with 7+ sessions booked in the first month. The Prayer Requests became a viral element, with 66% of increase sharing and request from February.

Calendly Implementation Details:

  • Integrated with Dr. Ross's existing Google Calendar
  • Added pre-session questionnaire to personalize meetings

Takeaways

  • Scheduling as Spiritual Care: The Calendly integration showed how technical solutions can directly support spiritual ministry needs.
  • Automation Enables Connection: By removing scheduling friction, we actually increased meaningful human interaction.
  • Prototype Testing is Crucial: Initial Calendly placement in the footer performed poorly - moving it to the main navigation increased bookings by 65%.
  • Technical Solutions Require Training: Created detailed documentation for Dr. Ross to manage her availability and booking settings.

Future Improvements

  • Group Sessions: Expand Calendly to offer small group prayer sessions
  • Automated Reminders: Integrate SMS reminders with spiritual encouragement
  • Post-Session Follow-up: Automated email sequence with resources discussed
  • Testimonial Wall: Showcase answered prayers to strengthen community faith
